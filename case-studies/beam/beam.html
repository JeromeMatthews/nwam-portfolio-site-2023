CLIENT DETAILS

Satus: Complete

Browne Entertainment Arts and Media - B.E.A.M Is a full-service professional performing arts center, dance company, talent agency, clothing line, and annual productions company headed by Ms. Sodanne Browne.

i

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

Built out the first iteration of the B.E.A.M group of companies’ sites. Main work focused on the company portal site. A hub to a proposed list of sub sites under the B.E.A.M umbrella company.

i

PROJECT DETAILS SUMMARY

TIMELINE: 2014 – 2015 | Q4 2014 - Q1 2015

Initial design draft was earlier in the year before summer of 2014. Later after the first draft was completed and stakeholder group review. A then final design was solidified prior to launch.

TIMELINE: 2015 | Q1 - Q2

Post site launch in late spring of 2015 we continued to provide maintenance and technical support for another 12-month period while the website still remained in our rotation of projects. Since 2017 overall site maintenance and development has been ported to other parties.
