PROJECT DESCRIPTION

The Clubs operate 3 branches serving over 400 athletes across the island of Jamaica.
The Head Coach and Managing Director Shin Nishida approached nomad with the request to update the layout, design, and content of the initial website built in 2010. read more >>

PROJECT DETAILS SUMMARY

TIMELINE: 2018 – 2022 | Q4 2018 – Q1 2019

Assisted review and updating of club sites to reflect the changes in requirements for the website’s daily functions. Also, a reduction of excess backend content management processes.

TIMELINE: 2016 – 2017 | Q3 – 2016

Company web presence design and expansion. Content Management System Integration and user training - (WordPress for base CMS and underscores for bespoke theme development)

MILESTONE DETAILS
AND DELIVERABLES

TIMELINE: 2016 – 2017

Q3 – 2016

MILESTONE:

First redesign of company site to improve all aspects of the site presentation, content layout, and expansion of programs to facilitate the growth of the group over the past five years - (at the time, from one main branch in Kingston, to 4 in different parishes across the island)

MILESTONE:

Initial build out and launch of Three (3) new branch sub-sites linked to the main branches’ portal site.

DELIVERABLE:

The redesign consisted of an overhaul of all the existing pages of content that were published and all the newly required content relevant to the expansion of the club’s program offerings. Through the addition of the sub-sites for the recently opened branches during the first expansionary period of the gym during 2014 – 2016, some 30+ pages of content were produced.

MILESTONE:

The creation of a back-end portal to allow for branch directors to independently update content to their branches, via a content management system (CMS) Utilizing a combination of WordPress and the bespoke theming tool underscores to create a solution that could best fit the needs of the gym.

DELIVERABLE:

Full port of the site redesign from Photoshop graphical design prototype -> HTML design -> PHP compatible code was done. The CMS required the HTML CSS and JavaScript code to be wrapped in a PHP file so they could be processed on the CMS’ engine. This allowed for a customized solution once launched using the underscores’ theme creation framework.

TIMELINE: 2018 – 2022

Q4 2018 – Q1 2019

MILESTONE:

Revamp of the company sites layout and design styles. The decision was made to revisit the company site to have a more streamlined and minimal presentation of the programs and services offered by the gyms.

DELIVERABLE:

Streamlined the content delivery process after assessing the usage of updates through the CMS portal. A new more minimal and clean site design was implemented to further refine the branding of the clubs, aiding to promote more consistency across all marketing channels.
